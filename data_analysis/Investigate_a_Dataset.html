Introduction¶

In this project we will be analyzing dataset of information about 100k medical appointments in Brazil. In particular, we will be interested in finding trends among the patients who did show up to appointment and how they differed from the patients who did not attend the appointment.

Problematic¶

Is the patient's age associated with attendance?
Is the received sms associated with attendance?
Is the handicap associated with attendance?
Which are the neighbourhoods which have most of the patient who didn't show up?

Conclusions¶

Summarizing the results we can highlight following points:
Age ist connected with attendance in different levels. It would be helpful to pay attention to patients aged between 10-35 and maybe remind them about meeting more frequently than other patients.
There are more patients who received a riminder sms didn't show up than the the patients who received an sms and did show up. This means that a second or third reminder may reduce non-attendance.
People with hadicap don't seem to be miss their appointments regularely. The visualizations and results show that the majority of handicap persons attend their appointments.
We have also seen the 14 neighbourhoods where we have most of not attended patients. Top 5 neighbourhoods of no-show patients are JARDIM CAMBURI, MARIA ORTIZ, ITARAR, RESISTNCIA, CENTRO.

Limitations: During the analysis we could face a problem of incompleteness of sms_received category. It could be useful to have more details about this category such as how many times and when were the reminders sent.
